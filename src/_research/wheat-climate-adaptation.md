At the CASSANDRA Lab in Milan, I worked on wheat under climate change across different countries, with a focus on what adaptation might actually look like once you leave broad statements and start working with data.

That meant dealing with historical weather records, future climate datasets in R, and stochastic weather generation for downscaled scenario analysis.

What I took from this project was a stronger interest in connecting climate data work to concrete agricultural choices rather than treating projections as an end in themselves.
