This is the work I keep coming back to: how to make crop models and climate information useful for actual agricultural decisions, not just for reports.

I use DSSAT, APSIM, STICS, seasonal forecasts, and environmental datasets to ask fairly practical questions about risk, timing, and adaptation in farming systems under climate variability.

What interests me most here is the translation problem: taking technical modelling work and turning it into something a farmer, advisor, or planner could plausibly use.
